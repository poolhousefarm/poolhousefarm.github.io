About Us

Pool House Farm is a small, family-run riding school and livery yard located in Balsall Common.

We are one of the only riding schools in Britain to specialise in teaching Mounted Games as well as offering a wide range of other equine activities for all ages.

What We Offer

Here at Pool House Farm, we are happy to cater for all abilities and ages. No matter if you have been riding for years or are only just beginning, you will be delighted by how much you will learn in the cheerful, relaxed environment that we provide.

Whether you want to jump, school, dabble in dressage or a mixture all, we have the horses and facilities to suite you. As well as this, we have the equipment to teach and play a variety of mounted games. Hacking is also available on the quiet roads surrounding the farm.

Own a Pony for a Day

Having a pony of their very own is one of the most exiting ideas for many children. We hold pony days during school holidays for any child who wants to experience this. Arrive in the morning with a packed lunch and spend the whole day with one of our ponies, learning how to care for him or her.
