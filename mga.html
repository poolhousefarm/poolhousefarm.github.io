Mounted Games Association

Mounted Games is a unique equine sport that rewards practice, teamwork and sportsmanship. Games riders and there ponies make skilled teams who learn to work with a large variety of equipment; everything from socks to traffic cones.

How Can Mounted Games Improve Your Riding?

Apart from being great fun, Mounted Games improves your riding skills in subtle ways that you may not notice, but will make a huge difference to your riding. Balance, co-ordination, position and control are naturally built up.

We use games regularly in our lessons, as it is one of the most enjoyable ways to learn to ride. Anyone can learn and enjoy!
